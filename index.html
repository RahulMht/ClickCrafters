TikTok integration for AI assistants

Manage your TikTok, straight from your AI assistant.

clickcrafters securely connects your TikTok account to an AI assistant (such as Claude) so you can review your content, check analytics, and publish posts — all through a simple chat.

Secure sign-in

Connect using TikTok's official login. You approve exactly what clickcrafters can access, and can revoke anytime.

See your content & stats

Pull your profile, video list, and performance metrics so your assistant can answer questions about how you're doing.

Publish & schedule

Ask your assistant to post or schedule a video to your own account — nothing happens without your explicit request.

How it works

  1. Connect your TikTok account through TikTok's secure authorization.
  2. Ask your assistant to show your videos, stats, or drafts.
  3. Tell it what to post — clickcrafters sends it to TikTok for you.
